# Terminology (locked)

Use this vocabulary consistently in UI copy, docs, and agent context.

## What this app does

**FrogsWork** is for **outgoing sales invoices**, money **you charge customers** (accounts receivable / AR).

| Use this | Meaning |
|----------|---------|
| **Sales invoice** | A tax invoice you **send to a customer** for work or goods you sold |
| **Outgoing invoice** | Same as sales invoice, invoice **from you to them** |
| **Accounts receivable (AR)** | Money **owed to you** after you invoice a customer |
| **Customer** | Someone you invoice (buyer / client) |

## What this app does **not** do

Do **not** describe the product as handling supplier bills, purchase invoices, or money you owe others.

| Avoid implying | What it actually is | This app |
|----------------|-------------------|----------|
| **Incoming invoice** | A bill a **supplier sends you** | ❌ Not supported |
| **Supplier bill / purchase invoice** | What you **pay** a vendor | ❌ Not supported |
| **Accounts payable (AP)** | Money **you owe** suppliers | ❌ Not supported |

If a feature is about tracking **sent / paid** status, it always refers to **your sales invoices**, not bills you receive.

## Two different “invoices” in the commercial product

| Term | Who sends it | Purpose |
|------|--------------|---------|
| **Sales invoice** (user) | You → your customer | Bill your customer for your work |
| **Platform fee invoice** (KorraOne) | KorraOne → you | Bill you for FrogsWork usage fees |

In UI and docs, say **platform fee invoice** (or **platform fees**) when talking about KorraOne charges, not plain "invoice" alone.

## Standard invoice wording (keep)

On a sales invoice PDF, **“Bill to”** means the customer you are billing. That is normal AR language, not accounts payable.

## UI label guidance

| Screen | Preferred label / hint |
|--------|------------------------|
| Home → create | Create sales invoice |
| Past Invoices | Sales invoices you’ve sent |
| Dashboard | Sales invoice totals |
| Customers | People you invoice |
| Settings → account | Platform account / platform fees (not “billing” alone if ambiguous) |
