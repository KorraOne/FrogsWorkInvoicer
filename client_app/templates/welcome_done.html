You can create sales invoices now. You only need an account above $2,000 ex GST invoiced in a month.

Your data on this PC

Settings, customers, and invoices are saved in %APPDATA%\FrogsWork\. PDFs go in the pdfs folder you chose at install (or the default under AppData). Change it anytime in Settings → Data storage.

If you remove an invoice from your list, the billing record stays and the PDF moves to a hidden .deleted folder in AppData, not your PDF folder.

If you create an account, only your email, hashed password, and usage billing sync to the KorraOne server. Customer details, line items, and PDFs stay on this PC.
