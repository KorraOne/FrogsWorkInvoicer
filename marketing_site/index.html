What it does

Accounts receivable: outgoing sales invoices you send to customers. Track sent, paid, and overdue. Your records sync through FrogsWork Cloud.

Create sales invoices

Line items, GST, due dates, and tax invoice PDFs to email or download.

Track customers and status

Customer list, outstanding totals, mark invoices sent or paid.

Browser, phone, and Windows

Same Cloud app everywhere. Optional Windows shell if you prefer a desktop window.

Account and subscription

Subscribe on the website. Stripe handles payments and promo codes.

Not accounts payable. FrogsWork is for invoices you send to customers. It does not track bills you receive from suppliers.
