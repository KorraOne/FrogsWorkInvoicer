What it does

Accounts receivable: outgoing sales invoices you send to customers. Track sent, paid, and overdue invoices. PDFs and customer records stay on your PC.

Create sales invoices

Line items, GST, due dates, and tax invoice PDFs to email or print.

Track customers and status

Customer list, outstanding totals, mark invoices sent or paid.

Works offline

No account during the trial. Subscribers can invoice offline. The app checks your subscription periodically.

Account and subscription

After the trial, subscribe via the app. Stripe handles payments.

Not accounts payable. FrogsWork is for invoices you send to customers. It does not track bills you receive from suppliers.
