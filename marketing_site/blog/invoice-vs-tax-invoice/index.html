Invoice vs tax invoice in Australia

People often say “invoice” for any bill. In Australian GST practice, a tax invoice has specific information requirements when you are registered for GST. Always confirm current ATO rules for your situation.

In FrogsWork

If your business is marked GST registered and the invoice includes GST, the PDF uses the Tax Invoice label. If you are not charging GST, you still get a clear sales invoice PDF for your records.

Quotes are different

Quotes and price estimates are not invoices. They set expectations on price before work; convert to an invoice when the job is accepted.
