Prepare invoices for tax time

Tax time is easier when invoices are already sent, paid dates are marked, and you can hand your accountant a clear pack.

Why this matters

Australian sole traders often need to provide a clean record of income for the financial year. Having tidy invoice history — with paid dates and correct GST treatment — saves time and reduces stress at EOFY.

Year-round habits

  • Email invoices promptly and mark them paid when funds arrive
  • Keep customer and ABN details accurate on your business profile
  • Use due dates so unpaid work is visible on the dashboard

Tax time export

In the Cloud app under Settings → Account, Tax time export builds a ZIP for a chosen Australian financial year:

  • an income ledger CSV
  • a short summary
  • matching invoice PDFs

It is separate from Download my data.

Common mistakes to avoid

  • Forgetting to mark invoices as paid (paid dates matter for bookkeeping)
  • Mixing GST and non-GST line items incorrectly
  • Editing business details mid-year without updating old invoices
  • Sending quotes instead of invoices for completed work

For GST field setup, see GST invoicing in Australia.
