How to turn quotes into invoices

Some jobs need a price before the work starts. FrogsWork keeps quotes optional so the app stays simple until you need them.

Turn Quotes on

In the Cloud app, open Settings → General and enable Quotes. A Quotes tab appears between Home and Invoices.

Send the quote

Create a quote or price estimate, then email the PDF. Quote emails do not include how-to-pay details.

Convert when accepted

Open the sent quote and choose Create invoice. Review totals, then send the tax invoice with payment reference and bank details if you use them.

FrogsWork does not send automated quote reminder emails. Use days-since-sent on your Quotes list to chase yourself. More detail: quote software for Australia.
