Pricing

No subscription. Platform fees only apply when you invoice more than the free allowance in a calendar month.

Free allowance

$2,000/month ex GST

  • No platform fee within the allowance
  • No account required
  • Works offline on this tier
  • Resets each calendar month

Above the allowance

0.05%

  • Fee on sales invoiced above $2,000 ex GST in the month
  • Formula: 0.0005 × (monthly total − 2000)
  • KorraOne account required before generating more invoices
  • Optional monthly cap to limit fees

Platform fee invoices (KorraOne → you)

Usage fees are billed separately from sales invoices you send customers. KorraOne sends a platform fee invoice for unbilled months. Default cycle is quarterly; annual is available in Settings. Each usage month appears on one platform invoice. Pay by bank transfer or PayID.

Estimate fees in the app under Settings → Fee calculator.
