Pricing

Start free. Subscribe in the app when you need more. Your invoice data stays on your PC.

Free trial

$0

  • 20 invoices or $20,000 ex GST (lifetime)
  • No account required
  • Works offline

Monthly

$12.99/month

  • Unlimited invoicing after trial
  • FrogsWork account + Stripe subscription
  • Offline with periodic subscription check

Annual

$129.90/year

  • Same unlimited access
  • 2 months free vs paying monthly
  • Manage billing in the app or Stripe portal

How billing works

  1. Download and install FrogsWork
  2. Use the free trial with no account
  3. When ready, open Subscribe in the app and pay via Stripe in your browser
  4. Create your password in the app; Stripe email is used for your account

Subscription checkout runs inside the desktop app, not on this website.
