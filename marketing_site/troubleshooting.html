Support · Using FrogsWork

Troubleshooting

Try these before you contact support.

  1. Restart FrogsWork. Close it from the taskbar if needed, then open it from the Start menu.
  2. Check you're online if you need sign-in, sync, or invoicing after the free trial (20 invoices or $20,000 ex GST).
  3. Check your version in Settings → App update (1.1.0 or newer).
  4. Find your data. Settings and invoices are in %APPDATA%\FrogsWork\. PDFs are in your pdfs folder (Settings → Data storage).
  5. Sending invoices. Use Prepare to send, copy the email text, and open the PDF yourself. Explorer won't open automatically.
  6. Still stuck? Contact support with your FrogsWork and Windows versions.
