Privacy policy

Last updated: June 2026. FrogsWork is operated by KorraOne.

Local data on your computer

FrogsWork stores customers, sales invoices, settings, and PDFs on your PC, primarily under %APPDATA%\FrogsWork\. This data stays on your machine unless you choose to back it up or sign in to a KorraOne account.

When you create an account

If you register, we store your email, hashed password, and usage billing records on the KorraOne billing server so fees can be calculated and platform invoices issued. Invoice PDFs you generate for customers are not uploaded unless you use a feature that explicitly sends data to the server.

What we do not do

  • We do not sell your personal information.
  • We do not use third-party advertising trackers in the desktop app.
  • We do not require an account for use within the free monthly allowance.

Updates and downloads

The app may contact the billing server to check for updates and download new versions from this website. Download requests use standard HTTPS.

Support

For access, correction, or deletion requests related to account data, contact us via KorraOne support.
