Privacy policy

Last updated: July 2026. FrogsWork is operated by KorraOne.

Cloud service

FrogsWork is a Cloud product. You use it in the browser, as a phone PWA, or through the Windows desktop shell. Those clients talk to the FrogsWork account API at api.frogswork.com.

Account and billing

When you register, we store your email and a hashed password. Stripe handles subscription billing. We link your account to a Stripe customer ID and subscription status.

Invoice and business data

With an active Cloud subscription, invoice records (business details, customers, invoice JSON) are stored in our Cloudflare D1 database, scoped to your account. Invoice PDFs are stored in our Cloudflare R2 bucket. Email sending uses Resend through our API.

Data is protected by HTTPS in transit and account access controls. Clients may keep a local cache (for example IndexedDB in the browser, or WebView2 storage in the desktop shell) to work when briefly offline.

Desktop shell

The Windows installer is a thin shell around the Cloud app. It may keep window size, update state, and an install identifier under %APPDATA%\FrogsWork\. It does not replace Cloud storage for invoices.

Anonymous usage statistics

The desktop shell may send anonymous usage information to our servers, for example that the app was installed, aggregate invoice counts (not invoice content), app version, and whether a subscription is active. We use a pseudonymous install identifier until you sign in. After you create an account, we may link that identifier to your email for support and billing only.

What we do not do

  • We do not sell your personal information.
  • We do not use third-party advertising trackers in the Cloud app or desktop shell.

Updates and downloads

Clients contact api.frogswork.com for sign-in, entitlements, documents, and email. Installers download from downloads.frogswork.com over HTTPS.

Retention and rights

We keep account and Cloud document data while your account is active and as needed for billing and legal obligations. For access, correction, or deletion requests, contact us via FrogsWork support.
