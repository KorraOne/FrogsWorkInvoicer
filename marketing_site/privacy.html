Privacy policy

Last updated: June 2026. FrogsWork is operated by KorraOne.

Local data on your computer

FrogsWork stores customers, invoices, settings, and billing cache on your PC, mainly under %APPDATA%\FrogsWork\. PDFs go in a pdfs folder, by default under AppData or wherever you chose at install or in Settings.

If you remove an invoice from your list, FrogsWork keeps a minimal billing record and moves the PDF to a hidden .deleted folder under AppData, separate from your PDF folder.

This data stays on your machine unless you choose to back it up or sign in to a KorraOne account.

When you create an account

If you register, we store your email, hashed password, and usage billing records on the KorraOne server so fees can be calculated and platform invoices sent. Customer invoice PDFs are not uploaded unless a feature explicitly sends data to the server.

What we do not do

  • We do not sell your personal information.
  • We do not use third-party advertising trackers in the desktop app.
  • We do not require an account during the free trial (20 invoices or $20,000 ex GST).

Updates and downloads

The app may contact the billing server to check for updates and download new versions from this website. Download requests use standard HTTPS.

Support

For access, correction, or deletion requests related to account data, contact us via FrogsWork support.
