Privacy policy

Last updated: June 2026. FrogsWork is operated by KorraOne.

Local data on your computer

FrogsWork stores customers, invoices, settings, and subscription cache on your PC, mainly under %APPDATA%\FrogsWork\. PDFs go in a pdfs folder, by default under AppData or wherever you chose at install or in Settings.

If you remove an invoice from your list, FrogsWork keeps a minimal record and moves the PDF to a hidden .deleted folder under AppData, separate from your PDF folder.

This data stays on your machine unless you choose to back it up or sign in to a FrogsWork account.

When you create an account

If you register, we store your email and hashed password on the FrogsWork account API (api.frogswork.com). Stripe handles subscription billing. We link your account to a Stripe customer ID.

Local vs Cloud storage

Local plan: Your invoice data (customers, line items, PDFs) stays on your PC under %APPDATA%\FrogsWork\ unless you export a backup yourself.

Cloud plan: Invoice records (business details, customers, invoice JSON) are stored on our Cloudflare D1 database, scoped to your account. Invoice PDFs are stored in our Cloudflare R2 bucket. The mobile browser app and Cloud desktop mode sync through the same API. Data is protected by HTTPS in transit and account access controls.

Anonymous usage statistics

For Local plan users, FrogsWork is built to keep your business data on your computer. To improve the app, it may send anonymous usage information to our servers. For example: that the app was installed, how many invoices you have created in total (a count, not the invoices themselves), your app version, and whether you have an active subscription.

We use a pseudonymous identifier for installs that have not signed in. If you create an account, we link that identifier to your email for support and billing only.

Local plan — we do not receive invoice content:

  • customer names, contacts, or ABNs
  • individual invoice line items, descriptions, or invoice numbers
  • invoice PDFs or attachments
  • bank account or BSB details on invoices (subscription payments are handled by Stripe)

Local-plan customers and invoice content stay on your PC unless you choose Cloud sync or back up data yourself.

What we do not do

  • We do not sell your personal information.
  • We do not use third-party advertising trackers in the desktop app.
  • We do not require an account during the free trial (20 invoices or $20,000 ex GST).

Updates and downloads

The app may contact api.frogswork.com for sign-in, subscription checks, and update metadata. Installers download from downloads.frogswork.com over HTTPS.

Support

For access, correction, or deletion requests related to account data, contact us via FrogsWork support.
