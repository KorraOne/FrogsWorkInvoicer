Privacy policy

Last updated: June 2026. FrogsWork is operated by KorraOne.

Local data on your computer

FrogsWork stores customers, invoices, settings, and subscription cache on your PC, mainly under %APPDATA%\FrogsWork\. PDFs go in a pdfs folder, by default under AppData or wherever you chose at install or in Settings.

If you remove an invoice from your list, FrogsWork keeps a minimal record and moves the PDF to a hidden .deleted folder under AppData, separate from your PDF folder.

This data stays on your machine unless you choose to back it up or sign in to a FrogsWork account.

When you create an account

If you register, we store your email and hashed password on the FrogsWork account API (api.frogswork.com). Stripe handles subscription billing. We link your account to a Stripe customer ID. Customer invoice PDFs are not uploaded to our servers.

Anonymous usage statistics

FrogsWork is built to keep your business data on your computer. To improve the app, it may send anonymous usage information to our servers — for example, that the app was installed, how many invoices you have created in total (a count, not the invoices themselves), your app version, and whether you have an active subscription.

We use a pseudonymous identifier for installs that have not signed in. If you create an account, we link that identifier to your email for support and billing only.

We do not receive — and cannot see — your:

  • customer names, contacts, or ABNs
  • individual invoice line items, descriptions, or invoice numbers
  • invoice PDFs or attachments
  • bank account or BSB details (subscription payments are handled by Stripe)

Your customers and invoice content stay on your PC unless you choose to back them up yourself.

What we do not do

  • We do not sell your personal information.
  • We do not use third-party advertising trackers in the desktop app.
  • We do not require an account during the free trial (20 invoices or $20,000 ex GST).

Updates and downloads

The app may contact api.frogswork.com for sign-in, subscription checks, and update metadata. Installers download from downloads.frogswork.com over HTTPS.

Support

For access, correction, or deletion requests related to account data, contact us via FrogsWork support.
