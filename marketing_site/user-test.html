Scenario A: New invoice

You run Greenfield Café (your business). You finished a gardening job for a customer, Riverside Office, at 8 Harbour Street, Fremantle WA 6160. They owe you $300 plus GST ($330 total). Create and save an invoice, then open the PDF.

Your business: Greenfield Café (GST registered)

Customer: Riverside Office, 8 Harbour Street, Fremantle WA 6160

  1. In FrogsWork, set up or confirm your business as Greenfield Café, GST registered
  2. Home → Create sales invoice
  3. Add customer Riverside Office with the address above
  4. Add a line item for $300 ex GST (total $330 with GST)
  5. Under Payment due, change Due date rule to something other than the default (for example End of next month). Check the due date preview updates.
  6. Click Review invoice, save, and open the PDF. Confirm the due date looks right.
  7. Return to this page when finished

Stuck on GST? Open Business details and choose Yes, registered for GST, then try again.

Scenario B: Send it

Riverside Office wants the invoice by email. FrogsWork helps you prepare the message; you send it from your own email app (this is intentional for the test).

  1. Open Prepare to send for your Riverside Office invoice
  2. Copy the email text and attach the PDF (Show PDF in folder or Copy file path)
  3. In your email app, send to crombie@korraone.com with subject test - and then the subject line from the app (for example test - Invoice 001 from Greenfield Café)
  4. Back in FrogsWork, click Mark as sent
  5. Return to this page when finished

Scenario C: Fix business details

Update Greenfield Café address and ABN in Business details if they still show your setup values:

Change your business details to:

  • Address: 15 Creek Road, Fremantle WA 6160
  • ABN: 51 824 753 556

Invoices you already made keep their old PDF. New invoices use the updated details (you will check in Scenario D).

  1. Home → Business details
  2. Update address and ABN, then save
  3. Return to this page when finished

Scenario D: Add logo

Add the Greenfield Café logo (your business) and confirm it shows on a new invoice to Riverside Office.

Download Greenfield Café logo

  1. Home → Business details
  2. Upload the logo and turn on Show logo on invoices, then save
  3. Home → Create sales invoice (any made-up job for Riverside Office)
  4. Save and view the PDF to check the logo appears
  5. Return to this page when finished

Scenario E: Past invoices

Riverside Office paid you. Mark the invoice as paid in your records.

  1. Home → Past invoices
  2. Find your Riverside Office invoice (the $330 one)
  3. Click Mark as paid
  4. Return to this page when finished

Scenario F: Dashboard

Check that your totals look right after marking the invoice paid.

  1. Home → Dashboard
  2. Check Outstanding and Paid totals (amounts should include GST where you charged it)
  3. Note anything that looks wrong
  4. Return to this page when finished

Uninstall FrogsWork

Close FrogsWork completely, then remove it so your PC stays clean.

  1. Right-click FrogsWork in the Start menu → Uninstall (or Settings → Apps → Installed apps → FrogsWork → Uninstall)
  2. Follow the prompts. FrogsWork saves your invoice PDFs to your Downloads folder so you do not lose them.
  3. Check Downloads for a FrogsWork-Uninstall-... folder. Delete it if you want those test files gone.
  4. Confirm FrogsWork is gone from the Start menu
