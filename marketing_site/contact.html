Support · Using FrogsWork

Contact support

Email crombie@korraone.com with a short description of the problem.

Include:

  • FrogsWork version (Settings → App update)
  • Windows version (Settings → System → About)
  • Whether you're signed in
  • What you were doing when it failed

Don't send customer names, invoice amounts, or PDFs unless we ask. Invoice data stays on your PC.

Support development

Platform fees apply when you invoice above $2,000 ex GST in a month and have an account. Tips help fund hosting and development. Contact us for PayID or donation options when listed.
