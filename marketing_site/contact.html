Support · Contact

Contact support

Email crombie@korraone.com with a short description of the problem.

Include:

  • Whether you use the phone app, browser at app.frogswork.com, or the Windows desktop app
  • Windows version if you use the desktop app (Settings → System → About)
  • The email you sign in with (not your password)
  • What you were doing when it failed

Don't send customer names, invoice amounts, or PDFs unless we ask. Your Cloud invoices stay in your account; we only need enough detail to reproduce the issue.
