Billing and your data

In the Cloud app, open Settings → Account.

  • Cancel or change plan — Manage subscription (Stripe Customer Portal).
  • Download my data — ZIP for your own records (not re-importable into FrogsWork).
  • Delete all my data or Delete account — permanent removal from FrogsWork Cloud; cannot be restored.
